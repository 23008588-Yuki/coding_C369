Live Market Feed

Description:

The Live Market Feed provides real-time updates on different currencies. User can view live data, including exchange rates, price changes, and market trends.

How to Access the Live Market Feed:

  1. Sign Up/Log in: Create an account or log in to your existing account on our platform.
  2. Navigate to Exchange page: Go to the 'Exchange' tab on the main menu.
  3. Payment: Complete the payment process using your preferred payment method (credit card, ApplePay, etc).
  4. Access: Once payment is confirmed, you will have full access to the Live Market Feed.

Top-Up feature

Description:

The Top-Up Feature allows users to add funds to their account seamlessly, ensuring they have the necessary balance to trade.

It allows for:

  • Instant top-up of account balance.
  • Multiple payment methods available (credit/debit card, etc).
  • Transaction history and balance tracking.

How to Use the Top-Up Feature:

  • Sign Up/Log in: Create an account or log in to your existing account on our platform.
  • Navigate to Top-Up: Go to the 'Wallet' section.
  • Choose Amount: Select the amount you wish to top up.
  • Select Payment Method: Choose your preferred payment method (credit/debit card, etc).
  • Two Factor Authentication (2FA): Enhance security
  • Complete Transaction: Your account balance will be updated instantly.
